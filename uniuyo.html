Computer Science

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science. The department contributes to the mission and vision of the University in training of undergraduate and graduate students with requisite skills needed to solve complex technological problems of modern society and the attainment the millennium development goals.

About Us

Computing science focus includes Software engineering, Soft computing, Computational Intelligence, Communications Networks and Theoretical Computing. The department offers degree programs leading to the B.Sc, M.Sc. in Computer Science and Ph.D. in Computational Intelligence.

Admissions Requirments

Admission by UTME into B.Sc. Programme Candidates wishing to enroll in the B.Sc. Degree programme in Computer Science must obtain at least five 'O' level credit passes in English Language, Mathematics, Physics plus two other Science subjects in the Senior School Certificate Examination (SSCE). Candidates must also sit for and pass both the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME) conducted by JAMB and the Post UTME screening test conducted by the University of Uyo. The subjects required in the UTME include English Language, Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry.

Objectives

The philosophy of the Department is centered on producing adequate and efficient manpower in the field of Computer Science and Information and communication technology through effective teaching methods and collaborative/ interdisciplinary research. The programme is designed to solve the immediate needs of the society as well as train personnel that will graduate to fit perfectly well into the knowledge based economy either as a player or a contributor as well as being readily employable.
